Competitor Products

We researched three primary competitors in the energy-tracking and sustainability engagement space:

1. JouleBug: A gamified sustainability app focused on personal habits. Great UX but lacks institution-scale energy data integration.

2. EnergyCap: Offers robust building-level utility tracking for universities. However, it’s more admin-facing and lacks student engagement.

3. Lucid BuildingOS: Visualizes real-time energy use in buildings but doesn’t offer gamified or mobile-first experiences.

ecoWattch blends real-time energy tracking with student-facing gamification — something none of the current competitors fully offer. We aim to create a unique experience that not only tracks energy usage but also encourages students to engage with their dorm's energy consumption in a fun and interactive way.

Technologies Used

Our project integrates a modern stack optimized for performance, scalability, and engagement:

• Frontend: Android Studio (Java)
• Backend:Microsoft Azure, AWS RDS MySQL
• Notifications System:Push Notifications
• Codebase & Version Control: Git + GitHub
• UI Design: Figma (Mobile-first + Gamified)

This tech stack supports our real-time leaderboard, dorm activity tracking, and personalized energy feedback system.
