02
The Solution
Our project aims to develop a mobile application that provides real-time energy consumption insights and actionable recommendations. This app will integrate with Willow's data platform to display energy trends, send alerts for peak consumption, and gamify energy-saving behaviors among students and staff. By leveraging real-time data, the app will drive awareness and influence sustainable habits.
03
Our Motivation
Sustainability is a growing concern, and we believe technology can empower individuals to make a difference. By providing real-time data and incentives, we hope to encourage energy-efficient behavior in university communities. This project aligns with global efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and supports institutions in lowering their energy costs.
04
Expected Outcome
Increased awareness of energy consumption and emissions impact. Behavioral changes that lead to more energy-efficient practices. Reduced energy costs for Our institutions Measurable reduction in carbon emissions through informed decision-making. A scalable app that can be adapted for broader sustainability initiatives.
